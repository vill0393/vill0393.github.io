About me

Since I was a young child I developed a love for the arts, particularly drawing
which led me to study Fine Arts in my home country. After graduating I worked
primarily in the printing industry as a graphic designer. Some years after, the
oportunity to move to Canada showed up.

It was in this country that I realized I needed to gain a fresh perspective on the
design industry, so I took the decision to enrolle in the Interactive Media Design
Program at Allgonquin College. After school, I hope to succeed as a freelancer
and continue to do the things I love.
